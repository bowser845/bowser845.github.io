the aviary studio

hand weaving selvedge denim from waste

The Aviary Studio is a small hand weaving studio and educator focussed on traditional denim weaving.

To read more and follow the journey click here, or head over to Instagram.

In our digital and industrial age, we're doing our best to preserve the ancient craft of hand weaving, celebrate its cultural importance and history, and teach others the value of knowing where our clothes come from.

Aviary Denim is supported by the Theo Moorman Trust for Weavers, and The Experimental Weave Lab.
